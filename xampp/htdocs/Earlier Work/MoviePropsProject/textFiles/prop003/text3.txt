A special effects version of the standard issue EXO suit rifle used in the production of 2014 sci-fi action film Edge Of Tomorrow. Summarily demoted and sent to the front lines, former propaganda officer William Cage (Tom Cruise) finds himself repeating the same disastrous day of combat over and over again. Enlisting the help of Sergeant Rita Vrataski (Emily Blunt), the soldiers struggle to find a way to bring the Mimics down and prevent the fall of the human race.
